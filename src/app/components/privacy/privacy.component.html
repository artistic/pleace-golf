OVERVIEW

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you visit https://pleacegolf.world (the “Site”).

We use your Personal Information only for providing and improving the Site. By using the Site, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.



PERSONAL INFORMATION WE COLLECT

When you visit the Site, we automatically collect certain information about your device, including information about your web browser, IP address, time zone, and some of the cookies that are installed on your device. Additionally, as you browse the Site, we collect information about the individual web pages or products that you view, what websites or search terms referred you to the Site, and information about how you interact with the Site. We refer to this automatically-collected information as “Device Information.”

We collect Device Information using the following technologies:

  1. “Log files” track actions occurring on the Site, and collect data including your IP address, browser type, Internet service provider, referring/exit pages, and date/time stamps.
  2. Additionally when you play in a tournament or attempt to play in a tournament through the Site, we collect certain information from you, including your name, email address, password, nationality, country of residence, handicap index, home club, gender, course and tee. We refer to this information as “Tournament Information.”
  3. When we talk about “Personal Information” in this Privacy Policy, we are talking both about Device Information and Tournament Information.

HOW DO WE USE YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION?

We use the Tournament Information that we collect generally to fulfill any tournament entries through the Site (including processing your entry, tournament scoring, tournament leaderboards, rankings, tournament results and providing you with confirmations). Additionally, we use this Tournament Information to:

  1. Communicate with you
  2. Screen our tournament entries for potential risk or fraud
  3. We use the Device Information that we collect to help us screen for potential risk and fraud (in particular, your IP address), and more generally to improve and optimize our sit

SHARING YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION

We share your Personal Information with third parties to help us use your Personal Information, as described above.

Finally, we may also share your Personal Information to comply with applicable laws and regulations, to respond to a subpoena, search warrant or other lawful request for information we receive, or to otherwise protect our rights.


YOUR RIGHTS

If you are a European resident, you have the right to access personal information we hold about you and to ask that your personal information be corrected, updated, or deleted. If you would like to exercise this right, please contact us through the contact information below.

Additionally, if you are a European resident we note that we are processing your information in order to fulfill contracts we might have with you (for example if you enter a tournament through the Site), or otherwise to pursue our legitimate business interests listed above.


DATA RETENTION

When you enter a tournament through the Site, we will maintain your Tournament Information for our records unless and until you ask us to delete this information.


MINORS

The Site is not intended for individuals under the age of 18.


CHANGES

We may update this privacy policy from time to time in order to reflect, for example, changes to our practices or for other operational, legal or regulatory reasons.
